Privacy Policy

LavaCaveBot (“the Bot”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

  • Data Collected: The Bot only processes the information necessary to respond to your commands (such as floor/version selections). It does not store message content permanently.
  • Storage: Temporary data (like images or poll votes) may be stored in memory while the bot is running, but no long-term storage is used unless specified by server admins.
  • Sharing: The Bot does not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • User Rights: If you want data related to you to be deleted, contact the maintainer on Discord and it will be removed promptly.

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Bot after updates constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
